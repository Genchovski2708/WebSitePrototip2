Partners

Realizing the common goals and tasks to which the partner organizations are directed, namely the reduction of economic, social, educational and cultural differences in society, by providing a high level of research and expertise for local and regional actors in the field of social development as citizens with the same human rights, our involvement is imposed not only temporarily while the project lasts, but to give our contribution to the continuity and sustainability of such projects that lead to the improvement of civil society at all levels (horizontal and vertical).

Our intention also includes equivalence in the methodology and the chosen methods that are designed and agreed upon with the partners based on the professional, practical experience of each of the partners and the real needs of the target group, thus ensuring the successful implementation of all project activities.

In that way, the partnership ensures the strengthening and sustainability of the project, because it creates new bases for creating a model with the possibility of combining the best practices needed at the local and regional level, in a unique way it will be reflected at the global level. The project partnership is structured to ensure that best practices, experience, models and lessons learned can be effectively and efficiently adapted to develop a sustainable approach to project implementation.
